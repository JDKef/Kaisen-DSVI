# Kaisen DS6 demonstration checklist

- [ ] Install the provided APK or open the app from Flutter.
- [ ] Register a new identifiable test user, or log in with that user's account.
- [ ] Create a clearly named test product with stock and a barcode.
- [ ] Edit the product and verify the saved values.
- [ ] Open **Registrar venta** and scan the product barcode.
- [ ] Register a sale with a quantity greater than one.
- [ ] Return to inventory and verify the expected stock reduction.
- [ ] Open the sales history and verify the sale line and quantity.
- [ ] Refresh the relevant screens and confirm the remote values remain visible.
- [ ] Close and reopen the app.
- [ ] Verify that the authenticated session is restored.
- [ ] Log out and confirm the login screen returns.
